Who are we?

Established in 2000, Breadtalk has been serving many different kinds of pastries and bread that has been in the favour of many generations of Singaporeans.

We have since been well known to create many different types of bread and pastries, including our iconic Flosss bread, which has been popular amongst our customers.

With the dedication and hard work of our staff and our quality ingredients, we have since revolutionised the way bread is being made, growing into a homely icon with our customers.

Our best seller

Our best seller is the iconic Flosss bread, which many Singaporeans come to love due to its fluffy texture and sometimes sweet and spicy taste.

It has since grown into a popular favourite amongst many Singaporeans.

This product has also inspired the creation of our beloved mascot, "Song Song"!
